About Me

This is Mithilesh, an Automation Engineer with 5.5 years of experience, currently working as a DCS Engineer (Siemens PCS 7) in the chemical industry. With a Master’s in Automation (Germany) and a Bachelor's in Instrumentation & Control, I’ve grown from field instrumentation to PLC, HMI, and DCS systems along the ISA-95 automation pyramid. Passionate about Industry 4.0, I’ve also worked on OT & IIoT, AR & VR projects and aim to integrate advanced technologies like AI to enhance system availability, safety, energy efficiency, and data security.

I am enthusiastic about working in collaborative team environments, continuously honing my managerial and leadership skills, and driving collective success.​​ I look to collaborate with like minded professionals to learn and contribute in my field.
